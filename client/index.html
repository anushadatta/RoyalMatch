RoyalMatch

In mathematics, economics, and computer science, the Gale–Shapley algorithm (also known as the deferred acceptance algorithm) is an algorithm for finding a solution to the stable matching problem. This implementation of the Gale–Shapley algorithm aims to find stable matching for the following 4 Kings and 4 Queens given their preferences.

> To specify preferences for each King and Queen use the given drop down menu.
> Ensure that each King or Queen has 4 unique preferences [♣, ♦, ♥, ♠], which may be listed in any order of preference.
> Please note that the algorithm will not work if the preferences for each Royal are not unique.

Preference 1 Queen ♣ Queen ♣ Queen ♣ Queen ♣
Preference 2 Queen ♦ Queen ♦ Queen ♦ Queen ♦
Preference 3 Queen ♥ Queen ♥ Queen ♥ Queen ♥
Preference 4 Queen ♠ Queen ♠ Queen ♠ Queen ♠
